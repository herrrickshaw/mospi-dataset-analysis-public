Errata & data caveats
  • WPI's April 2026 reading (167) is a ~4% one-month jump after four quarters near 154–158 — verify against an official MoSPI/RBI release before citing.
  • February 2026 IIP and CPI both show mild non-monotonic wobbles state-to-state; treat single-month moves as provisional pending revision.
  • RBI foreign exchange reserves lag roughly a year in this connector — the latest row returned was June 2025 even when 2026 was explicitly requested.
  • CPI year-on-year inflation is only computable from January 2026 onward in the connector's base-year-2024 series, since that series has no 2024 comparison months.
